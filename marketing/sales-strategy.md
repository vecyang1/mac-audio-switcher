# AudioSwitchPro Sales Strategy

## Pricing Strategy

### Gumroad Pricing (Recommended)
- **$9.99** - Premium positioning
- **$4.99** - Sweet spot (recommended)
- **$14.99** - Bundle with future apps

### China Market Pricing
- **¥30-50 CNY** ($4-7 USD) - Affordable for Chinese developers
- Gumroad accepts Alipay/WeChat Pay
- Consider regional pricing adjustments

## Sales Channels

### 1. Direct Sales (Keep 100%)
- **Gumroad** - Primary platform
- **Paddle** - Alternative with better analytics
- **Your own website** - Full control

### 2. Mac App Store ($99/year)
**Pros:**
- Built-in discovery
- Trust factor
- Automatic updates
- App Analytics

**Cons:**
- 30% commission
- $99 upfront cost
- App Store review process
- Sandboxing requirements

### 3. Alternative Monetization
- **GitHub + Donations** - Build reputation first
- **Freemium Model** - Basic free, pro features paid
- **License Key System** - Direct distribution

## Sales Funnel
```
YouTube/TikTok (vibe coding) → 
Twitter (dev tips) → 
Gumroad page → 
$4.99 purchase
```

## Gumroad Page Optimization

### Must-Have Elements
- GIF demo showing instant audio switching
- "Perfect for developers/content creators who..."
- 30-day money-back guarantee
- Early bird discount code (first 100 customers)
- Customer testimonials
- Clear feature list with benefits

### Psychological Triggers
- **Social Proof**: "Join 500+ developers who..."
- **Urgency**: "Launch price ends soon"
- **Authority**: "Built by developer for developers"
- **Reciprocity**: Free audio setup guide with purchase

## ROI Calculations

### Single App
- At $4.99: Need 20 sales to break even on App Store
- At $9.99: Need 10 sales to break even
- Direct sales: Keep 95% (after payment processing)

### Portfolio Approach
- 3-4 apps at $2.99-4.99 each
- Cross-promotion between apps
- Bundle pricing for multiple apps
- Only need ~10 total sales/month to profit

## Launch Strategy

### Pre-Launch (Week -2 to 0)
- Build email list with "early access"
- Create buzz with preview videos
- Reach out to Mac YouTubers/bloggers

### Launch Week
- Special launch pricing (30% off)
- Product Hunt submission
- Reddit announcements
- Twitter thread with full story

### Post-Launch
- Regular updates (drives visibility)
- User feature requests
- Case studies
- Affiliate program setup
